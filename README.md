## ğŸ’¡ Key Insights

* **Discounts do not significantly increase average purchase value.**
  Customers without discounts tend to spend slightly more per transaction.

* **Customer purchasing behavior is stable across all frequencies.**
  In every purchase frequency group, non-discount purchases dominate.

* **Clothing is the top-selling but most volatile category.**
  Strongly affected by seasonality and trends.

* **Footwear and Outerwear are the most stable categories.**
  Sales are consistent across seasons, making them low-risk inventory items.

* **Seasonality affects specific categories, not all products.**

---

## ğŸ§  Business Recommendations

* Use **discounts selectively** (customer acquisition or seasonal campaigns), not as a core revenue strategy.
* Focus **seasonal promotions on Clothing**, where impact is highest.
* Maintain **consistent stock levels for Footwear and Outerwear**.
* Shift strategy toward **customer experience and product value**, not price competition.
